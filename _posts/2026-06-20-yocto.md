Yocto is a DIY recreation of the Roland TR-808 drum machine, faithfully reproducing its iconic analog sound and circuit design. It adds modern features such as MIDI connectivity, individual instrument outputs, and an enhanced step sequencer.

> This design require a lot of efforts with sourcing and assembling.
