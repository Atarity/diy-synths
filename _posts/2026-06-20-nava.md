This is an exact reproduction of the analog part of the TR - 909 (with the exception of the BA662 VCA Clap has been replaced by a BA6110) coupled with a MIDI sequencer. You can store up to 128 patterns organized into 8 banks of 16 patterns in a Non-volatile EEPROM.

> This design require a lot of efforts with sourcing and assembling.
