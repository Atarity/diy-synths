32 programmable knobs and up to 4 macros per knob. High resolution up to 4095 steps. Quick presets, etc.

The N32B is built to handle a wide range of MIDI messages:
- Control Change
- Program Change
- RPN
- NRPN
- Aftertouch
- High-Resolution MIDI
- System Exclusive (SysEx)
