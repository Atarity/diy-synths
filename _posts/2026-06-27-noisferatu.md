Noisferatu is a noise and texture synthesis engine for the Seeed Studio XIAO SAMD21 microcontroller. 45 algorithms across 5 banks produce crackling textures, noisy scapes, evolving drones, harmonic blips and digital chaos. Shaped but never fully controlled by four knobs.
